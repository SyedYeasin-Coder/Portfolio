Who Am I?
Hi, I’m Syed Yeasin, a Computer Science Engineering student with a strong passion for game development, web development, and graphic design. I’m proficient in tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Adobe Animate, which have been instrumental in bringing my creative ideas to life. Combining my design expertise with programming knowledge has empowered me to tackle innovative projects and explore endless possibilities in the digital world.
